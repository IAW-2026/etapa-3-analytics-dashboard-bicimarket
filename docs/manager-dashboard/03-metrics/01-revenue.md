# 3.1 — Revenue Metrics

> **Manager Dashboard — Metrics & KPIs**
>
> Detailed specification of revenue-related KPIs, their formulas, data sources, and dashboard representation.

---

## R1 — Gross Merchandise Volume (GMV)

**Definition**: Total value of all approved payments in a given period. The primary measure of marketplace transaction volume.

**Formula**: `SUM(payments.amount_cents) WHERE status = approved`

**Data Source**: `GET /api/v1/payments` — `amount_cents`, `status`, `created_at`

**Aggregation**:
| Granularity | Display | Example |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| Real-time (today) | Large number + sparkline | ARS 1,234,567 |
| Daily | Bar in time-series chart | ARS 450,000 |
| Weekly | Bar in time-series chart | ARS 3,200,000 |
| Monthly | Bar in time-series chart | ARS 12,800,000 |

**Format**: Currency (ARS), whole numbers, no decimals. Large numbers formatted with commas.

**Dashboard Widget**: KPI card with trend indicator (↑12% vs previous period)

**Priority**: P0

**Notes**: GMV includes payment amount + shipping costs paid by buyer. It represents total marketplace transaction value, not marketplace revenue.

---

## R2 — Daily Revenue

**Definition**: GMV for a single day. Used for day-to-day tracking.

**Formula**: Same as R1 filtered to `DATE(created_at) = target_date`

**Data Source**: `GET /api/v1/payments?from=today&to=today`

**Dashboard Widget**: Time-series chart (last 7/30/90 days) + current day big number

**Priority**: P0

---

## R3 — Revenue by Seller

**Definition**: Total gross revenue generated by each seller's products.

**Formula**: `SUM(settlements.gross_amount_cents) GROUP BY seller_profile_id`

**Data Source**: `GET /api/v1/settlements` filtered by date range, grouped by `seller_profile_id`

**Dashboard Widget**: Horizontal bar chart ranking top N sellers + table

**Priority**: P1

**Limitation**: Requires iterating settlements per seller or having an aggregation endpoint. The dashboard can group by `seller_profile_id` if the settlements endpoint returns all settlements (not scoped to a single seller).

---

## R4 — Average Order Value (AOV)

**Definition**: Average amount per approved payment.

**Formula**: `AVG(payments.amount_cents) WHERE status = approved`

**Data Source**: `GET /api/v1/payments`

**Dashboard Widget**: KPI card with trend + time-series chart

**Priority**: P0

---

## R5 — Marketplace Commission Revenue

**Definition**: Total fees earned by the marketplace from settlements.

**Formula**: `SUM(settlements.fee_amount_cents) WHERE status = paid`

**Data Source**: `GET /api/v1/settlements` — `fee_amount_cents`

**Dashboard Widget**: KPI card + cumulative chart showing revenue over time

**Priority**: P0

**Note**: This is the actual revenue for the marketplace business. Fee is 10% of gross amount per documented settlement calculation.

---

## R6 — Effective Take Rate

**Definition**: Actual percentage the marketplace retains from each transaction.

**Formula**: `SUM(fee_amount_cents) / SUM(gross_amount_cents) * 100`

**Data Source**: `GET /api/v1/settlements`

**Dashboard Widget**: Percentage display with comparison to nominal rate (10%)

**Priority**: P1

**Note**: Effective rate may differ from nominal 10% due to refunds, partial settlements, or manual adjustments.

---

## R7 — Revenue Growth Rate

**Definition**: Percentage change in GMV between two periods.

**Formula**: `(current_period_gmv - previous_period_gmv) / previous_period_gmv * 100`

**Data Source**: `GET /api/v1/payments` (two date range queries)

**Dashboard Widget**: Percentage badge (green for positive, red for negative) on KPI cards

**Priority**: P0

**Period Comparisons**:
- vs período anterior (7 días): current 7 days vs previous 7 days
- vs período anterior (mensual): current month vs previous month
- Year-over-Year (YoY): current period vs same period last year
